This interactive visualisation is a representation of the "Glossary of terms commonly used in the #DataCreativities collaboration" document produced by the #DataCreativities team. The goal of this visualisation is to be an interactive glossary - providing a different way of engaging in the glossary content.

This is a living visualisation and will be iteratively updated.

Please cite this document as:

#DataCreativities (2020): An Interactive Glossary of terms commonly used in the #DataCreativities collaboration. https://doi.org/10.26188/13337915
